Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Our Policy

We offer a 7-day refund policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, contact us within 7 days of your purchase date and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

How to Request a Refund

Contact us via GitHub with your order number (found in your Lemon Squeezy receipt email). Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

Exceptions

Refund requests submitted more than 7 days after purchase will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Questions

For any questions about our refund policy, contact us via GitHub.
